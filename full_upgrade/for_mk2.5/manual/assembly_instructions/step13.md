# Assembly Instructions

## Step 13

### Parts

* 1x lcd_support_a
* 1x lcd_support_b
* 4x end_caps
* 2x M5x10
* 8x M5 set screws
* 2x Tee nuts


### Assembly

1. Mount the new lcd supports to the screen cover
2. Assemble them to frame with 2 M5x10 and 2 tee nuts
3. Assemble the end_caps and secure them with M5 set screws
