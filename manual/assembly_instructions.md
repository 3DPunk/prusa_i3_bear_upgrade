# Prusa i3 Bear Upgrade Manual

## Assembly Instructions

### Step 1

#### Parts

2x 195mm V-Slots
1x 270mm V-Slots
2x 90째 plates
8x M5x10mm screws
2x Black angle corner
4x M5x8mm screws
14x Tee nuts

#### Assembly

1.  Assemble 90째 plates to the v-slots with 8x M5x10 screws and 8 tee nuts and make sure everything is square (fig 1.1)
1.  Add 4 tee nuts on the 270mm v-slot as seen on fig 1.1
1.  Add 2 black angle corners and screw with M5x8 screws and 1 tee nut on each 195mm v-slot (fig 1.2)

### Step 2

#### Parts

2x feet
2x M5x15mm screws
2x Tee nuts

#### Assembly

1. Lay down the frame on a flat surface with 90째 plates facing the sky
1. Push down on each corners to check the twist of the frame (fig 2.1). If it wobbles, slightly loosen the 90째 plates screws (but not the black angle corner!) and twist the frame until it is flat (you can use an object under a corner to help twisting the frame)
1. Once the frame is flat, tighten strongly everything and add the feet using M5x15 and two tee nuts (fig 2.2)
